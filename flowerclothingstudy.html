Flower Clothing Study

In my pre-junior year of college at Drexel University, I was assigned to draw a idea for five days straight. My idea ended up being about flower-inspired clothing. I feel that I did a decent job with my theme this week, though I feel that towards the end I just used colors of the flowers instead of the flower itself. It was kind of hard towards then end since I was running out of ideas in terms of dresses so I ended up using the color scheme. Despite that, I feel satisfied with my dresses and their design.
